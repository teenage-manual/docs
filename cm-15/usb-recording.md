title: "usb-c recording"
category: "recording"
---

record directly via usb-c:

1. connect to computer/phone
2. select cm-15 as input device
3. record in your audio software

advantages:
- digital signal (no conversion loss)
- high-quality ADC built-in
- class compliant (no drivers needed)
- works with phones/tablets

best quality option for most uses
