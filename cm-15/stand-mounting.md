cm-15 mounting solutions:

built-in stand:
- fold out from bottom
- rest on any surface
- angle as needed

tripod adapter:
- included ¼" and ⅜" adapter
- fits standard mic stands
- compatible with tripod mini

shock mounting:
- helps reduce vibrations
- recommended for sensitive recording
- third-party mounts compatible
