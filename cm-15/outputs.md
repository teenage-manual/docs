---
title: "output connections"
---

cm-15 has 3 simultaneous outputs:

usb-c:
- digital audio to computer/phone
- highest quality
- also charges the mic

mini XLR:
- professional connection
- use included adapter cable
- for mixers/interfaces

line out (3.5mm):
- analog stereo output
- for recorders/cameras
- dual mono signal

use any combination simultaneously
