cm-15 - portable condenser microphone:

1. charge via usb-c (2 hours for full)
2. flip power switch to ON
3. green LED = powered and charged
4. connect to your recording device

features:
- large 1-inch diaphragm
- 3 outputs (usb-c, mini XLR, line out)
- 10 hour battery
- built-in table stand
