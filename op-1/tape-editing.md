---
title: "Tape Editing"
category: "tape"
---

edit your tape recordings:

lift (cut):
hold [shift] + press [rec] to cut selection

drop (paste):
hold [shift] + press [play] to paste

split:
hold [shift] + [<] or [>] to set region markers

select region:
hold [shift] + turn blue encoder

reverse:
hold [shift] + press [stop] to reverse playback
