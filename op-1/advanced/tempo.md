---
title: "Tempo and Sync"
---

set and sync tempo:

1. hold [tempo] to see BPM
2. turn blue encoder to adjust
3. tap [tempo] repeatedly for tap tempo

sync modes (in tempo screen):
- beat match: sync to incoming audio
- PO sync: sync with pocket operators
- MIDI sync: sync via MIDI

hold [shift] + [tempo] to access metronome
