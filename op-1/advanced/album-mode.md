---
title: "Render to Album"
---

export your song as an audio file:

1. press [mixer] then T4
2. hold [shift] + [rec] to start rendering
3. press [play] - the tape plays and records to album
4. press [stop] when done
5. hold [shift] + [stop] to save

access album files via disk mode
files are saved as AIFF format
