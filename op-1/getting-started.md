---
title: "Getting Started"
category: "basics"
---

1. charge your op-1 via usb (5V only)
2. slide the power switch towards you to turn on
3. turn the volume knob (far left) to hear sound
4. press any key on the keyboard to play

the 4 main modes:
- [synth]: play synthesizer sounds
- [drum]: play drum kits
- [tape]: record and arrange
- [mixer]: mix your tracks
