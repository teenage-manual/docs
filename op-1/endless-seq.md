---
title: "Endless Sequencer"
category: "sequencing"
---

the endless sequencer is a looping recorder:

1. hold [shift] + turn blue encoder to select endless
2. press [rec] to arm recording
3. play notes - they loop automatically
4. press [rec] again to stop recording
5. sequence keeps playing

turn encoders to adjust:
- blue: loop length
- green: speed
- white: swing
- orange: note length
