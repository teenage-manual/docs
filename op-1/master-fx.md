title: "Master Effects"
category: "effects"
---

apply effects to your final mix:

1. press [mixer] then T3
2. hold [shift] + turn blue encoder to select effect
3. turn encoders to adjust parameters

effects include:
- delay, reverb, chorus
- filter, EQ
- phone (lo-fi)
- punch (compression)
- spring, CWO, grid
