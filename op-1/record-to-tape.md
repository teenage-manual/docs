---
title: "Record to Tape"
category: "tape"
---

1. press [tape] to enter tape mode
2. press T1, T2, T3, or T4 to select a track
3. press [synth] or [drum] to choose your sound
4. press [rec] then [play] to start recording
5. play the keyboard to record notes
6. press [stop] when done

the recording is now on tape
press [play] to hear it back
