creative tape techniques:

varispeed:
hold [shift] + turn green encoder to change tape speed
record at half speed, play back at normal for octave up

reverse:
hold [shift] + [stop] for reverse playback

loop:
set loop points with [shift] + arrow keys
hold [loop] while playing to loop section

tape speed affects pitch and tempo together
