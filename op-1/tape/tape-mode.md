---
title: "Tape Mode Basics"
---

press [tape] to enter tape mode

op-1 has a 4-track tape recorder:
- T1-T4 select which track to record to
- press [rec] + [play] to record
- press [play] to play back
- press [stop] twice to return to start

the tape is 6 minutes long
record synths, drums, or external audio
