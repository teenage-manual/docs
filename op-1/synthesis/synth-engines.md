---
title: "Synth Engines"
---

op-1 has many synth engines:

- dr wave: wavetable synthesis
- dsynth: digital FM-style
- cluster: granular textures
- digital: classic digital
- DNA: experimental
- pulse: pulse width
- string: physical modeling
- sampler: play your own samples

hold [shift] + press [synth] to change engine
use the blue encoder to browse
