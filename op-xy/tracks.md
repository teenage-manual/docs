---
title: "Working with Tracks"
category: "basics"
---

op-xy has 4 tracks (T1-T4):

each track can have:
- its own sound/instrument
- its own sequence
- step components for variation

press T1-T4 to select a track
link tracks to play them together

use track buttons to:
- mute/unmute tracks
- solo a track
- access track settings
