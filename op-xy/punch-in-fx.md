---
title: "Punch-In FX"
category: "effects"
---

add real-time effects while playing:

1. hold [FX] button
2. press keys to trigger different effects
3. effects are temporary while held
4. release to return to normal

each key triggers a different effect:
- filters, delays, reverbs
- stutter, glitch
- DJ-style effects

record punch-in FX to your performance
