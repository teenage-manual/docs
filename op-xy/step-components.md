---
title: "Step Components"
category: "sequencing"
---

step components add variation to steps:

types of step components:
- chance: probability of playing
- ratchet: note repeats
- velocity: dynamic changes
- pitch: pitch variations
- slide: portamento between notes

to add step components:
1. select a step
2. hold [shift] + turn encoders
3. different encoders add different components
