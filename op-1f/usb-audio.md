---
title: "Usb Audio Interface"
category: "connectivity"
---

use op-1 field as a usb audio interface:

1. connect to computer via usb-c
2. select op-1 field as audio device in your DAW
3. record directly into your computer
4. or play computer audio through op-1

4-channel output: tape tracks 1-4
stereo input: line in or internal sounds

great for recording to DAW or live performance
