title: "Fm Radio"
category: "advanced"
---

op-1 field has FM radio receive AND transmit:

to receive:
1. hold [mic] to access input sources
2. select FM radio
3. turn encoder to tune frequency
4. sample directly from radio

to transmit:
1. go to com mode
2. select FM transmitter
3. broadcast your music to nearby FM radios

great for sampling or wireless audio
