tx-6 has 6 x 3.5mm stereo inputs:

each input can be configured as:
- stereo (L+R)
- mono (summed)
- split (separate L and R)

connect:
- pocket operators
- op-1/op-z
- synthesizers
- phones/tablets
- any line-level source

guitars/mics may need a preamp
