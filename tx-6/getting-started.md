tx-6 - ultra-portable mixer:

1. charge via usb-c
2. flip power switch up
3. connect audio sources to inputs 1-6
4. adjust faders and select knob

features:
- 6 stereo inputs (12 mono)
- 3 outputs (main, cue, aux)
- built-in effects and EQ
- usb audio interface
