artist tracks -8 professional reggae/dub tracks with separated stems for remixing

what you get:
8 complete tracks from real artists, broken into stems

stems typically include:
- drums/percussion (group A)
- bass line (group B)
- melodic elements - keys, guitar, horns (group C)
- vocals and dub elements (group D)

what you can do with them:
1. mute/unmute stems to create dub mixes
2. add your own sounds on top
3. apply effects and filter sweeps
4. chop and rearrange sections
5. learn from professional arrangements

great for:
- understanding how dub mixing works in practice
- practicing live performance and remixing
- learning reggae song structure and arrangement

access artist tracks:
load the factory projects that contain them (check documentation for which project numbers)
