title: "Dub Siren"
category: "effects"

dub siren - the classic sound system effect, built right in

the dub siren is what makes a reggae sound system a sound system

activate:
press and hold the dedicated siren button on the device

while holding:
- x-knob: pitch/frequency (sweep up and down)
- y-knob: modulation speed (how fast it wobbles)

release to stop the siren

use it for:
- drops and transitions
- building tension before a drop
- classic dub style interludes
- DJ-style hype moments

pro tip: combine with the delay effect
- set up delay as your master effect
- hit the siren
- instant dub siren echoes fading into infinity
